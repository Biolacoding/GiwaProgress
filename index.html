Artist Statement

I am a contemporary visual artist dedicated to exploring environmental sustainability through recycled materials and marine pollution narratives.

My work transforms discarded plastics and waste into powerful artistic statements, creating a dialogue between art, environmental consciousness, and social responsibility.

"To create art that not only captivates but also catalyzes change, turning environmental challenges into opportunities for creative expression and community engagement."

Through my Recycle Art Tour initiative, I've engaged thousands of students and community members, demonstrating that art can be both beautiful and purposeful.
